# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in this project.

This is a **personal project** and is not open to external contributions. Pull requests from outside collaborators will not be reviewed or merged.

**Forking is welcome.** Feel free to fork this repository and adapt it for your own use under the terms of the [LICENSE](LICENSE).

If you find a bug or have a suggestion, you are welcome to open an issue, but please note that responses are not guaranteed.
